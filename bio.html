What I did before bootcamp

Prior to the bootcamp, I worked as a substitute teacher,
at different schools around Cincinnati. The subjects that
I had specific training for were Middle School Science and
Language Arts (grades 4-9), but due to the fluid nature of
substitute teaching, I taught a variety of topics.

Why I chose to do a bootcamp

While teaching would be my "first love" as a career choice, I
did not succeed in making the transition from the temporary
state of substituting to a full-time position. As I greatly
desired to have a stable career, I investigated options that
would let me utilize my fondness for computers, and help me
start a career. With the high ratings earned by Max Techincal
Training, I quickly signed up to study with the bootcamp.

Where I want to work after bootcamp

As of yet, I do not have a specific place in mind for where I
would like to work. There are some areas that could be interesting,
such as working with the Cincinnati Museum Center or City Hall, but
I am primarily searching for an environment where everyone has a
great deal of pride in their work.
